How to Use



What is the purpose?


This website provides an easy-to-use interface for people to find what they're looking for at a brick-and-mortar store near them at a glance.



How do I use it?


  1. You can start on the main page. Enter the your location or the address of where you would like to find store near. Enter any items you're looking for separated by a comma.
  2. Click the "Search" button and you'll be taken to a list of stores that match your search criteria by relevance or proximity to your chosen location.
  3. You can update your search by adding filters or updating the search items or location.
  4. Click to view details about specific stores to see what items they sell, their hours of operation, directions, and their phone number.
